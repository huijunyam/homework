Omlette

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Omlette is a ealthy and convenient food for beginner to learn to cook. Further information can be found here: Omlette Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • pinch salt
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1 / 2 teaspoon chopped chives

Directions

  1. Crack the warm eggs into a bowl, add salt and blend with a fork.
  2. Heat a 10-inch nonstick aluminum pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Once the pan is hot, add the butter and brush around the surface of the pan.
  4. Pour the eggs into the center of the pan and stir vigorously with a rubber spatula for 5 seconds.
  5. Using your spatula, move it around the edge of the egg mixture to help shape into a round and loosen the edge.
  6. Let the omelet sit in the pan for 10 seconds without touching.
  7. Slide the omelet onto a plate and fold over so that the omelet is a tri-fold.
  8. Coat with the remaining butter and sprinkle with the chives.
  9. Serve immediately.
